Interesting Facts About Harry Potter

Did you know that the first Harry Potter book, "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone," was published in 1997?

The Harry Potter series has been translated into over 80 languages, making it a global phenomenon.

Author J.K. Rowling came up with the idea for Harry Potter during a train journey!

Harry's lightning bolt scar is one of his most iconic features, and it's a result of a failed curse by Voldemort.

The Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is known for its four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin.
